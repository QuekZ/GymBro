Bench Press

3 sets × 8-12 reps
Starting Position
Focus: Upper chest development
Muscles: Pectoralis Major, Triceps, Anterior Deltoids
Equipment: Barbell, Bench
Difficulty: Intermediate
Instructions:
  1. Lie on a flat bench with your feet planted firmly on the ground
  2. Grip the barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width
  3. Lower the bar to your chest with controlled movement
  4. Press the bar back to starting position without locking elbows
Notes:

Maintain proper form, don't arch back excessively. Keep shoulder blades retracted throughout the movement.

⏱️ Rest: 60-90s between sets
⚖️ Suggested Weight: 70-80% of 1RM
